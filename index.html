Year: --------- 1898;
Location: ----- The World;

The world is taking its first steps into imperialism. Europeans have realized the need of colonies and protectorates in order to support rapidly growing, industrial countries. For most of the industrial powers, the view of the outside world is one of opperotunity. However, with European monastaries threatening their religeon and culture, many chinamen did not view imperialism quite as positively. China was not doing well at the time; There were droughts and famines, the economy had plummeted and the ruling dynasty had been overtaken by foreign powers.

Year: ---------- 1900;
Location: ----- ShanDong;

A small sect of chinamen forms on the ShanDong peninsula in northern china. Known as the Big Swords Society, this society was very ethnocentric, meaning that they rejected other cultures. This ethnocentricity probably was caused by the monasteries put in place be the europeans. The Big Swords Society gained influence when a local official called upon them to fight bandits in the area. After this, the Big Swords Society turned on local chinese christians and foreigners, burning a church.

This martial art society, and others like it, formed the precedent for the society known by americans as the Boxers. Originally the Boxers were known as the Society of the Righteous and Harmonious Fists, but they soon changed their name to the Militia United in Righteousness, to distance themselves from their cultist origins.

The Boxers saw the chinese chrisitans and europeans as foreign aggressors that needed to be removed.

Year: ---------- 1900;
Location: ----- Northern China;

The Rebellion truly began when the Empress-Dowager changed her policy toward the boxers. The Boxers assembled and marched on Beijing, burning churches and oppressing christians and foreigners The foreign Legations called for their respective countries to defend them. A force of French, Russian, British, U.S., German, Italian, Japanese and Austrian soldiers amassed in beijing to defend their monastaries. The Boxers were joined my the Chinese army and some muslim rebels.
